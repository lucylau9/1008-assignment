"Moon Adventure" Nominated for Award

Our game, titled "Moon Adventure", has been nominated for the Children's Web Based Adventure Game Award (CWBAGA). You play as an astronaut on their first space expedition to the moon. Along the way, you can choose to befriend or attack aliens, and the story changes depending on your actions. This game is full of twists and turns with a heartwarming story and multiple endings: available to purchase on multiple platforms!

Puzlr turns 7 years old

On 14th November, Puzlr celebrated its 7th birthday. During those 7 years, we have made over 10 unique games across a range of different categories. Notable ones include "Moon Adventure", "Create It" and our most recent release, "Our AI Future". Thank you to all who have supported us throughout these seven years.

Puzlr and AI

AI has become an increasingly popular topic, and something us at Puzlr are interested in. Our recent project: "Our AI Future" explores the implications of AI on humanity in a way that's easy and fun for children to understanding. Currently we are working on implementing more ideas of AI and even making our own chatbot.
